Our Story

From the Founders of Rye,
15 Romolo & The Cordial

Rye On The Road was founded by Greg Lindgren and Jon Gasparini, the team behind three of San Francisco's most beloved bars. We spent years building neighborhood institutions and learning what makes people feel genuinely taken care of. Then we put it all on wheels.

We serve events of every scale, from intimate gatherings of 50 to public events with over 100,000 guests. Whether it's a wedding in Marin, a corporate activation in SoMa, or a night market at Fort Mason, we show up with the same craft every time. Our commissary is rooted at 140 10th Street in San Francisco, the kitchen behind everything we pour.
