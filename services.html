Tier One

Streamlined
Service

Our Streamlined package is built for efficiency without sacrificing quality. Ideal for large-scale events, public activations, and high-volume settings where speed and consistency matter most.

We use batch cocktail techniques developed from years behind the bar, paired with carefully selected beer and wine. Compostable cups and serviceware keep things sustainable and easy to manage at any size. The result is a bar that moves fast and still tastes like it was made with care.

Full Bar
Service

The Full Bar experience is what we built our reputation on at Rye, 15 Romolo, and The Cordial. This is the complete package: a handcrafted cocktail menu designed around your event, a full spirits selection, made-to-order drinks, and premium glassware throughout.

We meet with you ahead of time to build something that feels like it actually belongs at your event. Whether that's a signature cocktail named after the couple, a menu that tells your company's story, or simply the best old fashioned your guests have ever had.
